Choose a Plan

Clients select from various plans based on their needs and budget

Payment

After selecting a plan, clients proceed to payment.

3

Onboarding and Brief

Upon successful payment, a project manager is assigned to the client for onboarding and task clarification. Clients fill out a brief detailing their business and design needs to help designers understand their requirements.

4

Designer Assignment

A personal designer is assigned to the client after the brief is completed.

5

Task Creation and Submission

6

Feedback and Revisions

7

Task Completion

Once the client is satisfied, the task is marked as complete.

After completing one task, clients can create new tasks and continue the process.
